🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Dictionary App · Last updated: April 2026

📋 Introduction

Welcome to Your Dictionary. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

This app is free to use and supported by advertisements. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

📊 Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Search queries: Words you search — stored locally on your device only
  • Favorites: Words you save — stored locally on your device only
  • App preferences: Theme, language settings — stored locally
  • Usage data: Anonymous statistics collected by Google AdMob
✅ We do NOT collect: your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any personal identifiers.

📡 Internet Usage

Your Dictionary uses the internet for:

  • Fetching word definitions from Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev)
  • Translating text via MyMemory Translation API
  • Displaying advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Loading "Word of the Day"

All API requests are made over secure HTTPS connections.

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads including:

  • Device advertising ID
  • Approximate location (country level)
  • App usage patterns

You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings: Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

📌 Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

💾 Data Storage

All user data (recent searches, favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by clearing app data in device settings
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

👶 Children's Privacy

Your Dictionary is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app contains educational dictionary content appropriate for all age groups.

🔄 Third-Party Services

  • Free Dictionary API — dictionaryapi.dev (no data collected)
  • MyMemory Translation — mymemory.translated.net (anonymous requests)
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Google Fonts — for typography

🛡️ Security

  • All external API calls use HTTPS encryption
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • No sensitive personal data collected or stored

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the date at the top. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
