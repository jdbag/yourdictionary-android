## 2. Information We Collect

**Your Dictionary does not collect any personal information.**

We do not collect, store, or share:
- Your name or identity
- Your email address
- Your location
- Your device identifiers
- Your search history

All your favorites, settings, and preferences are stored **locally on your device only** and are never transmitted to any server.

---

## 3. Internet Usage

The App uses internet access for the following features only:

- **Dictionary lookups** — via Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev)
- **Word translations** — via MyMemory public API (mymemory.translated.net)

These requests contain only the words you search for. No personal data is included or transmitted.
